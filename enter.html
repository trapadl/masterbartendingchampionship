MBC Membership Benefits

Free Membership

Anyone can join MBC free from anywhere in the world. As a free registered member you are:

  • Able to claim a profile on the leaderboard
  • Submit results for a cocktail competition for admin approval

Paid Membership

Paid members of the MBC are entitled to:

  • 10% off ticket entry for any MBC endorsed competition
  • 10% off merch and equipment from our online merch store
  • A members lapel pin, which admits discount to ticketed entries (special founders pin design for those who sign up before 28/4)
  • 1x Free entry ticket to each hosted MBC endorsed event for you or a friend
  • Access to members only nights at trap.
  • Access to Members only communication channels

You can become a paid member by buying an annual membership using the same email address you use to create an account or sign in above.
